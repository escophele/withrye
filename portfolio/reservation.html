What should I expect when I arrive at the store?
When you come in for your reservation, let a Specialist know you’re there to pick up your iPhone. Once you’ve checked in, we can help you with Personal Setup and answer all your questions. If you’re purchasing iPhone with a carrier plan, please allow at least 30 minutes to complete your purchase.
What is the iPhone Upgrade Program?
The iPhone Upgrade Program is a new way to own iPhone. It lets you choose your carrier and spread the cost over 24 low monthly payments. The iPhone Upgrade Program comes with the option to get a new iPhone every year and the security and protection of AppleCare+. *
Learn more
Can I change or cancel my reservation?
You can’t change or cancel your reservation. However, if you’d like to change the model you reserved, a Specialist can check the availability of a different iPhone when you come in at your scheduled time. Due to high demand, the availability of some models and reservation times may be limited.
What if I’m late for my reservation time?
If you’re late, or if you miss your scheduled time altogether, we may not be able to hold the model you reserved, and you may have to make another reservation. So please arrive as close to your check-in window as possible.
